Frequently Asked Questions

What do I need to rent a vehicle in Morocco?

To rent a vehicle through Vahanar, you'll need a valid driver's license (international driving permit for non-EU licenses), passport/ID, and a credit card for the security deposit. The minimum age is 21 years (25 years for premium vehicles).

How does payment and insurance work?

We accept credit cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. Basic insurance is included in all rentals, with options to upgrade to comprehensive coverage. Deposits are refunded within 7 days after vehicle return, pending inspection.

Can I book a one-way rental?

Yes, one-way rentals are available between major Moroccan cities for an additional fee. Simply select different pick-up and drop-off locations during booking.

What languages does the app support?

The Vahanar app is available in Arabic, English, French, Spanish, and German. Our customer support team can assist in these languages as well.
